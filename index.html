Research

Original Research

The Semantic Void: LLM-Enabled Insider Threats & the Failure of Traditional Security Controls
Independent Research · 2025 · Manuscript complete — available on request

Identified and characterized a novel class of AI-driven cyberattacks where insiders use LLMs to paraphrase sensitive data, bypassing traditional DLP, EDR, and DFIR controls entirely. Proposed SAITM — a semantic-aware defense framework currently being developed into a working proof-of-concept.

  • SAITM Framework: uses vector embeddings, cosine similarity, and intent-aware prompt analysis to detect meaning-preserving exfiltration at the semantic level
  • Comparative analysis across four defense paradigms over five security dimensions, citing 12 sources including 2025 arXiv papers and reports from Cyberhaven, Lakera AI, and Palo Alto Networks Unit 42
  • Forensic architecture: immutable logging layer establishing semantic chain of custody for AI-era incident response
